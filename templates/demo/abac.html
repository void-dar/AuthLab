🏷️ Attribute-Based Access Control Lab

Dynamic authorization using user attributes — department, clearance level, age, country, membership.

ABAC vs RBAC

RBAC grants access based on who you are (your role). ABAC grants access based on your attributes and context. ABAC enables fine-grained policies like: "Allow access if department=Finance AND clearance≥3 AND country=US" — impossible to express with roles alone.

📋 Policy Definitions

Financial Reports

department == "Finance" AND clearance_level >= 3

Only Finance department staff with at least level 3 clearance can view financial reports.

Adult Content

age >= 18

Age-gated content accessible only to users 18 and above.

US Geo-Restriction

country == "US"

Content licensed only for the United States (DMCA/licensing compliance).

Premium Features

membership_status == "premium"

Paid features restricted to premium subscribers.

Engineering Tools

department IN ["Engineering", "DevOps"] AND clearance_level >= 2

Internal developer tools accessible only to engineering staff.
